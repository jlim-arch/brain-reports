Bottom line

→
fomo's product is "social-first memecoin trading" — leaderboards of top traders, one-click copy trading, live trade overlays on price charts, and a two-sided referral program (referrer earns 25% of a friend's fees, friend gets 10% off). That's a strong growth engine for speculative multi-asset trading, but copy trading and leaderboard-driven speculation are the opposite of Blockstream's positioning — bitcoin-only, self-custody, "not your keys not your coins." Porting the mechanic wholesale would undercut the trust story that differentiates us (see the Fireblocks comparison). What's worth taking is the underlying primitives — public leaderboards, social proof, and a referral loop — applied to things we'd actually want people to be public and social about: verified bitcoin holdings, treasury transparency, and wallet-adoption referrals, not trade-copying.

What fomo actually does

Core mechanics

Social feed for following top traders; a leaderboard ranked by 24h/7d/30d returns showing win rate, total return, max drawdown, and asset mix. On a token's page, other traders' buy/sell actions are overlaid directly on the price chart.

Copy trading: pick a trader, allocate capital, and their trades auto-replicate proportionally in your account. Multi-chain (Solana, Base), gasless, Apple Pay funding, 0.5% transaction fee, instant email/Apple-ID onboarding.

Growth loop

Two-sided referral: referrer earns 25% of a referred friend's trading fees ongoing, the friend gets a 10% fee discount. Combined with public leaderboard placement (status-seeking) and cross-device continuity ("open on phone, close on desktop"), the loop is built to spread through trader circles, not paid acquisition.

Team is ex-dYdX/Uniswap/OpenSea; $19M total raised including a $17M Series A led by Benchmark, with Polygon/Solana-affiliated angels — credible operators applying DeFi/NFT growth patterns to memecoin trading.

What it means for us

The capture didn't specify which "Blockstream app" — Green (consumer self-custody wallet), the Jade companion experience, or the Enterprise/AMP side. The ideas below span all three; treat this as a menu, not a spec.

Ideas worth exploring

  • Transparency leaderboard, not a performance leaderboard. A public page ranking verified corporate bitcoin treasuries (opt-in, proof-of-reserves style) — replicates fomo's status/social proof loop without touching custody or trading.
  • Referral credit for Jade hardware / Enterprise intros instead of a trading-fee split — same two-sided growth shape, applied to a purchase or a qualified lead instead of a fee.
  • Onboarding speed benchmark. Time Green's current onboarding against fomo's "seconds, email or Apple ID" bar — this is the one mechanic that's a clean apples-to-apples UX comparison regardless of product category.
  • On-chain activity feed for Liquid/AMP, reframing fomo's live trade overlay into an informational "what's moving on Liquid right now" view for Enterprise prospects — content, not a trading signal.

Open questions

  • Is there appetite for anything referral/growth-loop shaped at all, given Blockstream's enterprise sales motion is relationship-driven rather than viral?
